# Genre Templates — Vocabulary Banks & Prompt Templates

## 1. Outdoor / Adventure

**Action verbs**: `charges`, `soars`, `conquers`, `navigates`
**Scale descriptors**: `vast`, `endless`, `towering`, `sweeping`
**Texture words**: `rugged`, `weathered`, `sun-baked`, `wind-carved`

**Sound design** (video): layered wind, crunching footsteps, bird calls, building orchestral score, breath sounds

**Image example**:

```
Aerial drone shot over Iceland during golden hour, majestic landscapes
and soft sun flares, cinematic camera sweep, slow motion, deep slate
and warm gold palette, Hasselblad H6D, deep depth of field
```

**Video example**:

```
Helicopter flyover of a rugged mountain trail at sunrise. A lone hiker
in a bright red jacket crests a ridge, wind whipping their hair. Camera
cranes up from behind to reveal an endless valley below shrouded in
morning mist. Orchestral strings swell. Golden hour light floods the
scene. Palette: deep greens, warm amber, cool mist blue.
```

## 2. Health / Wellness / Calm

**Gentle verbs**: `glides`, `unfolds`, `breathes`, `settles`
**Texture words**: `silky`, `smooth`, `dewy`, `translucent`
**Atmosphere**: `serene`, `meditative`, `still`, `calming`

**Sound design** (video): ambient water, soft piano, Tibetan bowls, gentle breath, minimal — silence is a feature

**Image example**:

```
Top-down view of spa elements: serum bottle, eucalyptus leaves, smooth
stone, rising steam. Soft spotlight on each item. Fine mist. Warm ambient
light through sheer curtains. Canon EOS R5, 50mm, shallow DoF, muted earth tones
```

**Video example**:

```
Slow cinematic shot of a frosted glass serum bottle on raw concrete as
soft light spills through a linen curtain. Camera glides sideways,
revealing condensation forming on the glass. Airborne mist in slow motion.
Light piano keys echo faintly. Palette: warm cream, soft grey, translucent white.
```

---

## 3. Futuristic Tech

**Precision words**: `sleek`, `sculpted`, `precision-engineered`, `seamless`
**Tech vocabulary**: `holographic`, `magnetic`, `biometric`, `modular`
**Motion**: `pulses`, `activates`, `deploys`, `locks into place`

**Sound design** (video): sub-bass hums, electronic chirps, sonar pings, mechanical snaps, servo whirs

**Image example**:

```
Camera rotates around a floating smartwatch above a magnetic field grid.
Blue light pulses beneath. Reflections ripple across the display. Pitch-black
background, single cool spotlight. Sony A7R IV, 85mm macro, extremely shallow
DoF, high contrast, chrome and midnight blue palette
```

**Video example**:

```
A chrome wireless earbud hovers in darkness. Camera cranes upward to reveal
soft blue light scanning its surface. The earbud emits a thin pulse wave.
Laser-thin light beams trace its contours. Sound: electric current hum,
distant crystalline chime. Palette: chrome silver, deep black, electric blue.
```

## 4. Cinematic Product Showcase

**Luxury words**: `opulent`, `exquisite`, `crafted`, `premium`
**Light words**: `catches`, `glints`, `refracts`, `shimmers`
**Motion**: `orbits`, `reveals`, `emerges`, `locks into position`

**Sound design** (video): rising synth pads, deep cinematic bass, material sounds (glass clink, fabric rustle), string swells

**Image example**:

```
Camera orbits a high-end fragrance bottle on glossy marble. Backlight creates
long shadows. Thin mist swirls at the base. Soft spotlight. Dark moody
background. ARRI Alexa aesthetic, 70mm, shallow DoF, warm amber and deep black
```

**Video example**:

```
A luxury watch rotates mid-air in zero gravity, camera tracking upward
from beneath. Brushed metal reflects cascading light trails. Time freezes
on 11:11. Audio: ticking morphs into cinematic heartbeat and reversed strings.
Single warm spotlight against pure black. Palette: gold, deep charcoal, warm white.
```

## 5. Fresh Market / Organic

**Sensory words**: `glistens`, `fragrant`, `crisp`, `sun-warmed`
**Natural**: `farm-fresh`, `handpicked`, `artisanal`, `organic`
**Warmth**: `golden`, `honeyed`, `buttery`, `sun-drenched`

**Sound design** (video): distant chatter, wooden creaks, soft acoustic guitar, birdsong, satisfying food sounds

**Image example**:

```
Macro shot of water droplets rolling down ripe heirloom tomatoes in a wooden
crate. Warm golden hour light from the left. Shallow DoF, sharp foreground.
Fujifilm X-T4, 60mm macro, Kodak Portra aesthetic, warm earth tones
```

**Video example**:

```
Camera drifts through a bustling open-air market under linen canopies.
Sunlight beams through gaps, casting patterned shadows on baskets of fresh
lemons and herbs. A vendor's weathered hands arrange figs on a wooden board.
Soft accordion music and distant chatter. Camera tilts up to catch golden
dust motes. Palette: warm gold, deep green, rustic brown.
```

## 6. Mood Board

**Prompt structure**:

```
multi-panel mood board [split into N boxes], [Brand/Theme],
seamlessly blend lifestyle images, color swatches, [product shots],
in a [clean/symmetrical] layout. [Color scheme], [aesthetic direction]
```

**Brand collaboration example**:

```
Multi-panel mood board split into 8 boxes, outdoor apparel collaboration,
seamlessly blend lifestyle images, color swatches, product shots, lifestyle
images in a clean layout. Earthy color scheme, rugged durability
```

**Cosmetics example**:

```
Design a mood board for a makeup brand drawing inspiration from nature's
palette. Focus on sunset, earth, and sand hues — deep terracotta to soft
golden beige. Highlight bronzers, warm eyeshadows, nude lipsticks. Incorporate
golden hour landscapes, sand and clay textures, and warm-toned swatches.
Radiate natural glow, organic beauty and simplicity.
```

**Interior design example**:

```
Create a look book for a Santa Fe furniture collection, adobe and teal
color palette. Blend rustic adobe textures with teal accents. Furniture
and decor showcasing warm earthy tones alongside vibrant teal. Traditional
style with modern twist, sleek lines and minimalist designs.
```

## 7. Food & Beverage Photography

**Prompt structure**:

```
[Shot type — top-down/close-up/table-view] of [dish description with
ingredients and plating], [Setting], [Lighting], [Photography style]
```

**Fine dining example**:

```
Invigorating top-down shot of a vibrant fine dining experience at an outdoor
Michelin-star restaurant. Navajo-Churro Lamb Carpaccio — thinly sliced heritage
lamb with juniper salt. Prickly pear gel, vibrant pink, wild arugula. Served on
polished obsidian slab. Innovative plating, outdoor rustic setting with bright
natural light and long shadows, award-winning food photography
```

**Cocktail example**:

```
Invigorating shot of a Cholla Blossom Margarita — tequila, triple sec, fresh
lime juice, cactus blossom syrup, served over ice in a glass rimmed with sea
salt and crushed red chile flakes. Garnished with lime wheel. Vibrant outdoor
fine dining, bright natural light and long shadows, award-winning food photography
```

**Trigger words**: `invigorating`, `award-winning food photography`, `innovative plating`
