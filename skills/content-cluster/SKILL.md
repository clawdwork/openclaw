## What It Produces

1. **Silo URL structure** — `/{category}/{subcategory}/{slug}/` hierarchy
2. **Pillar page spec** — 3,000-4,500 words with AI definition block
3. **12-20 article briefs** with UNIQUE H1, H2 outline, meta description per article
4. **Cannibalization check** — verifies zero duplicate headings across site
5. **H2 outline table** — every H2 for every article, no repeats
6. **LLM micro-summary blocks** — AI definition block per article
7. **12-week publication schedule** — max 2/week, phased rollout
8. **Interlinking map** — pillar ↔ articles ↔ product with anchor rotation
9. **Anchor text rotation** — 8-10 semantic variants, no exact-match repetition
10. **Spam risk checklist** — 7-point verification
11. **Traffic projection** — indexed pages, organic traffic, keyword coverage at 6mo
